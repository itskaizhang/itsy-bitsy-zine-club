The Itsy Bitsy Zine Club is a lil' creative community for anyone interested in making zines, prints, stickers, photos, or anything else your heart desires.

We offer a monthly subscription where members receive a curated zine bundle featuring work by our community—delivered straight to your residential college suite or off-campus housing, completely free of charge.

If you’re interested in creating zines with us or subscribing to our physical mailing list, please fill out this form. Vibes will be dope, trust.
